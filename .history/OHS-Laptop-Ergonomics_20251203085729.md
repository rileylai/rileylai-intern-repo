### **1. What are the risks of using a laptop without an external monitor or keyboard?**

Using a laptop alone often forces you to look downward and reach forward. This can lead to neck strain, rounded shoulders, lower-back pain, wrist discomfort, and eye fatigue. Over long periods, these poor postures increase the risk of repetitive strain injuries and long-term musculoskeletal problems.

---

### **2. What ergonomic equipment can improve posture when working on a laptop?**
Helpful equipment includes:
- Laptop stand  
- External keyboard & mouse  
- Ergonomic chair  
- Footrest  
- External monitor  
---

### **3. What adjustments should be made to monitor height, chair position, and desk setup for a healthier workspace?**
